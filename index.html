Product Description

↣ Compact Origami-Inspired Design: Using advanced origami folding techniques, our splint can be compacted to fit into small first aid kits, making it perfect for on-the-go emergency care.

↣ Chemically Activated Inflation and Cooling: The splint is inflated by a reaction between sodium bicarbonate and citric acid, creating carbon dioxide. This reaction also provides an endothermic cooling effect, reducing swelling and pain.

↣ Easy Application: With a back zipper design, the splint allows for quick and easy application, ideal for self-application in emergency situations.

↣ Durable Material: Made of high quality ripstop nylon, our splint is tear and puncture resistant, ensuring reliability in various conditions.
