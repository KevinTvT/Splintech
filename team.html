Mission Statement

"Our commitment to providing superior care and comfort equips first responders and individuals with the most innovative tool for immediate and effective injury treatment. Inspired by personal sports-related injuries and our research project on badminton injuries and personal, we aim to use our work with the Conrad Challenge to enhance the efficiency, accessibility, and effectiveness of first aid response, especially in critical and remote environments. By integrating advanced origami folding techniques and a unique chemically activated cooling system, we provide a compact, easy-to-use, and therapeutic solution for stabilizing injuries. "

Team Values

↣ Safety: We prioritize patient safety and comfort, focusing on efficient designs that reduce application time and maximize therapeutic benefits.

↣ Quality Care: Our goal is to improve outcomes in emergency situations by providing reliable, high-quality products that contribute to better patient care.

↣ Innovation: We are committed to continuous innovation, pushing the boundaries of medical technology to improve patient care and first aid practices.

↣ Accessibility: Our products are designed with accessibility in mind, ensuring they are easy to transport and use, making quality emergency care available in all settings.

↣ Sustainability: Environmental responsibility guides our choice of materials and processes, ensuring that our products are ecological and sustainable.

↣ Adaptability: We constantly adapt to the changing needs of the medical field, ensuring our products remain relevant and effective in various conditions.

↣ Awareness: We are dedicated to educating the public and the medical community about the importance of timely and proper injury management.

↣ Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration, working closely with medical professionals and first responders to develop solutions that meet real-world needs.
